The Journey
Month by Month
✉️
December 2025
Jack reached out to Coach T about being a TA — Coach T didn't have a class at the time.
Key Moment
Coach T invited Jack to a meeting about a brand-new class he was building: Field Experience.
They talked about Jack TA/interning for Coach T and helping shape the course from the ground up.
🚀
January 2026
Jack officially starts his internship with Coach T.
First assignment: design the Field Experience logo.
Begins collaborating on the first Field Experience presentation.
Key Moment
First meeting with Gary Ricke — the path to building the Field Experience website begins.
💻
February 2026
Gary teaches Jack and Coach T to build websites with AI — they're introduced to Claude, Netlify, Terminal, Cloudinary, and more.
Milestone
Jack, Coach T, and Gary launch the Field Experience website to Netlify — just the beginning.
Jack builds Coach T's full interview calendar and master scheduling list.
Spends the week running passes around school, tracking down students while Coach T grinds out back-to-back interviews.
🎬
March 2026
Big project: film and edit a video for the school board presentation where Coach T, Mr. Gamlin, and Ms. Savitski pitch Field Experience.
Jack interviews students from the Link and Incubator programs and edits everything together.
Stand-out Moment
Superintendent Tom Kim walks up to Jack by name and tells him the video was great — a moment Jack will never forget.
End of month: produces a second video — a fundraiser film for Incubator at Penrose, shown to local business leaders.
Stand-out Moment
At the event, people line up to tell Jack how great of a job he did.
📈
April 2026
Jack and Gary deepen their collaboration — Jack learns a tremendous amount from Gary's expertise.
Jack and Gary begin taking on projects together beyond Field Experience.
New Client
Jack is hired independently by another BHS teacher to produce a college application video — seniors sharing advice for future applicants.
Films, edits, and delivers the college video — it comes out great.
Takes on side work for Coach Piron whenever needed.
🏆
May 2026
New assignment: produce a Teacher Appreciation video — students share gratitude for BHS teachers.
Stand-out Moment
The video goes out to all BHS teachers for appreciation week. Multiple teachers personally reach out to thank Jack for making it.
Next project: a Future Educator video featuring BHS students on the path to becoming teachers.
The video is featured at the school's big Teacher Signing Day.
The Field Experience website nears completion — and it looks great.
The Payoff
Jack is now working for Gary Ricke and has a Field Experience internship lined up for next year — with an electrician.
